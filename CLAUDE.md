## The 5-Touch Sequence — Never Deviate
**Touch 1 — LinkedIn connection request**
No pitch. Just: "Fellow CSV practitioner — would love 
to connect." Personalized with one specific detail 
about their company.

**Touch 2 — Value message (3 days after connect)**
Share one insight relevant to their specific situation. 
No mention of EvoLV. Pure value. Practitioner to 
practitioner.

**Touch 3 — Soft problem probe (5 days later)**
"Quick question — how are you currently managing 
change impact assessment when a validated system 
requirement changes?" Listen. Don't pitch.

**Touch 4 — The EvoLV introduction (3 days later)**
Only after Touch 3 response. Connect their answer 
to EvoLV's specific solution. One sentence. 
Specific ask: "I have 20 minutes Thursday or Friday 
— worth a quick look?"

**Touch 5 — Final follow-up (5 days later)**
"Last ping on this — I'm selecting 3 design partners 
this month for early access. If timing's off, 
completely understand. If not, Thursday still works."
