### Fit Score: **COOL**

**Rationale:** Ligand is a royalty aggregator, not an operating pharma/biotech. At ~130 people with no internal manufacturing, their CSV footprint is minimal. The XOMA acquisition adds 7 commercial royalties and 100+ development programs, but those systems live at their partners, not at Ligand. A validated-state monitoring platform solves a problem Ligand doesn't directly own. Worth keeping on the list for a post-acquisition conversation (Q3 2026+) if the XOMA integration creates a need to track partner compliance, but not a priority for June 30.

**Exception to consider:** If Ligand has an IT compliance function managing systems for financial reporting, partner tracking, or regulatory submissions — a targeted conversation about those systems could work. But this is a stretch.
